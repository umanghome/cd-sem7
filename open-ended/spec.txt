List of commands:

VAR <VAR_NAME>
Creates a variable with name <VAR_NAME>

STO <VAR_NAME> <NUM>
Stores value of <NUM> in <VAR_NAME>

PRI <VAR or NUM>
Prints the value of <VAR or NUM> specified

ADD <VAR> <VAR or NUM>
Adds <VAR> and <VAR or NUM>, and stores it into <VAR>

SUB <VAR> <VAR or NUM>
Subtracts <VAR> and <VAR or NUM>, and stores it into <VAR>

MUL <VAR> <VAR or NUM>
Subtracts <VAR> and <VAR or NUM>, and stores it into <VAR>
